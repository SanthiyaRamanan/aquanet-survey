Introducing AQUANET 🌊
What is AQUANET?

AQUANET is an integrated digital platform combining marine datasets, interactive ocean maps, fisheries statistics, genomics & eDNA data, acoustic data, and a research-focused collaboration community — all in one place. It is designed to bridge data fragmentation, empower researchers, and support conservation decisions worldwide.
